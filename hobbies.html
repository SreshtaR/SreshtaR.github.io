My Hobbies!


What I do in my free time (not that I get a lot of it)

Chess


A board-game where two people go head to head, use their brain, come up with strategies and get a taste of war - Chess!


What's not to like about this? Picking others' brain and getting to flash my wit, I've been a fan of this game since my abc's. Be it playing at home with my cousin, or a tournament full of players (I've won a few), it never fails to make me feel proud of myself and learn new techniques.

DIY


Turning what others would call trash into treasure (and sometimes, well, just destroying something in an attempt to make something else)


Anyone who has known me since childhood would have a huge collection of "gifts" that I create(d) just for them. The journey started in "cute" birthday cards with spelling mistakes and is currently at fixing broken coffee mugs and
re-inventing stuff that I lay my eyes upon.

Music


There is sound, there is noise and then there is music. My definition of music - any noise/sound that doesn't get on my nerves.


This one is quite simple - my mom was playing some music and one fine day, the younger me (3 years old) sang along; She saw (or heard?) the "genius" in me and I started attending vocal classes. A fan of the R&B and EDM (yes, you read that right), music has been my friend ever since.
